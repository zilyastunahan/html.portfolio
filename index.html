I was responsible for designing furniture, lighting, and accessories using recycled materials with a focus on sustainability and traditional craftsmanship. The design studio blended my passion for sustainable design and product design. That allowed me to gain hands-on experience in the design process, from researching materials and creating prototypes to testing and refining the final product. Also I had a chance to explore art and music scene in Berlin.

Erasmus+ Exchange

I had the opportunity to participate in painting, sculpting, photography, and interaction design courses, which allowed me to develop my skills in visual communication and storytelling. These experiences have shaped my perspective on art and design. Furthermore, I had the chance to collaborate with artists and designers from diverse cultural backgrounds, allowing me to explore various disciplines. The most importantly, I gained a deeper understanding of Polish history and culture. Also I traveled to several countries in Europe and collected unforgettable memories

Europian Solidarity Corps

As a volunteer, I was responsible for creating visual materials for youth events, including poster designs, social media posts and logos. I also editing videos and photographs. Additionally, I led drawing and stencil making workshops for children in youth club. This experience helped me develop my interpersonal skills, including active listening in communication. Volunteering has given me a deeper appreciation for social values and the importance of being and active citizen.
